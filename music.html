Music

Piano, songwriting, and rock — a constant alongside my research.

Piano

I am a classically trained pianist, having played since I was five years old. Genres I enjoy playing include classical, jazz, soul, rhythm and blues, rock (classic, soft, progressive, modern), and folk.

Casey Hartman

I am currently writing my first solo album, Atlantic Divides. The goal of the album is to be a piano-forward, singer-songwriter record with themes touching on longing, distance, and love. All music and lyrics are written by me.

Bad Neighbours

Bad Neighbours is a rock band I was a member of in Boston, Massachusetts from 2024 to 2025. We played covers and original songs spanning classic, alternative, and indie rock as well as hip-hop. As a member of the band I played piano, sang backing vocals, and contributed to the songwriting process, writing both music and lyrics.

Jack's Cat

Jack's Cat is a rock band I co-founded in Newport, California in 2025. We played covers and original songs spanning classic and alternative rock as well as rhythm and blues. As a member of the band I played piano and shared lead vocals.
